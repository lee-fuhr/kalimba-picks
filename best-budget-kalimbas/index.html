Kalimbas have become a beloved entry point into music-making: they’re small enough to slip into a backpack, soothing to play, and forgiving for total beginners. The best budget models prove you don’t need to spend much to get a decent, tuneful instrument that holds its pitch for weeks. When shopping on a budget, focus on key count (10 vs 17 affects range), tine material (steel is standard), and body wood (solid mahogany resonates better than plywood).
